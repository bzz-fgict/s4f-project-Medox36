15-Puzzle
Game

aka 15er Spiel

A Simple 15-Puzzle game made with Java Swing.

To find out more about the game visit the Wikipedia site.

The Looks

The game is kept simple. There is a shuffle button at the top. To reshuffle the numbers for when you get stuck or want to restart the game. There is also a text which displays certain text for when a wrong move was made or when you finished the game.

For moving the numbers you press on a number next to the free space and the number you clicked on will be moved to the free space. No Drag and Drop!
