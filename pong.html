Pong

A Simple Pong game. Made with Java Swing.

The Looks

The game is kept simple. A score board at the top and the game board at the center.

The score goe up to the max value of an Integer, which is 2147483647. But I won't give a guarantee that the score at high numbers is displayed correctly or doesn't overlap

Visual Bugs

The Ball seems to be getting bigger and smaller when it is moving around. This is because the ball is a JPanel and Swing isn't really perfect for fast moving GUI Components.
